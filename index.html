About Me

I’m a passionate graphic designer based in Ottawa, focused on creating meaningful and visually engaging experiences. With a strong interest in UX/UI design, branding, and digital media, I love turning ideas into designs that feel both functional and expressive.

My work blends creativity with purpose — whether it’s designing immersive visuals, building user-friendly interfaces, or developing brand identities. I’m always exploring new techniques and pushing my skills to create designs that connect with people and leave a lasting impression.
